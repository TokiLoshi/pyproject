About B's Briefing

What is this site for?

Hi, I'm Bianca, but my friends call me B, and this is my Final Project for Harvard's CS50 Introduction to Programming with Python. Using the Beautiful Soup Library, I built a web scraper to pull headlines from various news sources and display them as a news dashboard on a simple flask app.

Why did you want to build a web scraper?

Web scraping is a powerful tool and is something I wanted to learn. I wanted to build a web scraper to solve one of my irritations in life, too many emails with news headlines. I wanted a site that would include news I don't have in one place, including news from South Africa, Mozambique, San Francisco, and Tech news. Having all of this in one place saves me time in the morning and keeps me up to date with industry, local and global events.

Why is a login necessary?

To reduce the number of calls to these sites and potentially get my IP blocked, I decided to make it necessary to log in to view the news headlines. I created a demo video on the home page to showcase how the site works. If you'd like to try it out, you are welcome to contact me and ask for visitor access.
